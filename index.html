About Us

K & T Transport Services is a professional transport service company based in Gauteng, South Africa. We specialize in providing safe and reliable transportation for learners, ensuring they get to and from school securely. With our fleet of well-maintained Toyota Rumion vehicles, we also offer Uber services during the day, maximizing the efficiency of our fleet.

Our Mission

At K & T Transport Services, our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation for learners, ensuring parents have peace of mind knowing their children are in good hands. Safety is our priority.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted transportation service in South Africa, known for our commitment to safety and customer satisfaction.
