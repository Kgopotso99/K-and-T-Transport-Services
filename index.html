About Us

K & T Transport Services is a professional transport service company based in Gauteng, South Africa. We specialize in providing safe and reliable transportation for learners, ensuring they get to and from school securely. With our fleet of well-maintained Toyota Rumion vehicles, we also offer Uber services during the day, maximizing the efficiency of our fleet and creating a dependable source of income for our drivers.

Founded with the aim to bridge the gap between affordable school transportation and the demand for reliable ridesharing, K & T Transport Services stands for quality, safety, and customer satisfaction. We are proud to serve the community and contribute to the growth and development of young learners by giving parents the peace of mind that their children are transported with care.

Our Mission

At K & T Transport Services, our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation for learners, ensuring parents have peace of mind knowing their children are in good hands. We also offer Uber services with a higher level of safety above what the app can offer. Your SAFETY is our business.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted transportation service in South Africa, known for our commitment to safety, efficiency, and customer satisfaction. We aim to expand our services to cover all major provinces in the future, offering a nationwide solution for learner transportation and ride-hailing services.

Our Values

  • Safety first: We prioritize the safety of every child and passenger we transport, and our employees.
  • Reliability: We strive to be dependable and ensure on-time pickups and drop-offs.
  • Integrity: We conduct business with honesty and transparency.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do.
  • Diversity: We value and embrace our differences.

Our Services

We provide the following services:

  • School transportation for learners, offering safe, affordable, and reliable services.
  • We also provide discounts for parents with at least 3 children.
  • Discounts are also available when the yearly transport fee is paid in advance.
  • Uber services: Our 7-seater Toyota Rumion fleet guarantees comfort, safety, and convenience. When you ride with our driver, you know who you are riding with.
  • Special arrangements for learners with extracurricular activities.

Note: Our services are currently available in Gauteng province, with plans to expand in the near future.
